Keywords

Use keywords to keep track of incoming messages and send automatic replies. Useful for event signups, Q&A's, etc.

Each keyword has a start and stop time and custom messages for respondants that are too early or too late.

Scheduled Messages

Schedule messages to be sent to contacts or groups.

Groups

Groups make it easy to keep in touch with Sunday School helpers, Small Group members or any other groups of people you may need to contact regularly.
